About The Project

Erasmus+ Short-Term Mobility Project

Sustainable Competencies in the Digital Era

This Erasmus+ project aims to improve the academic, professional and digital competencies of students and teachers.

Through international mobility activities, students and teachers will gain practical experience, strengthen language skills and develop a stronger European identity.

The project focuses on innovation, digital transformation and sustainable development in vocational education and training.

Project Objectives

Our main goals and expected achievements

Professional Skills

Improve students' vocational and professional competencies through practical experiences.

Academic Success

Increase student motivation, academic achievement and reduce school dropout rates.

Innovative Teaching

Enable teachers to use innovative educational methods and digital tools effectively.

Language Development

Strengthen foreign language skills through international cooperation and mobility.

European Awareness

Increase awareness of European identity, values and cultural heritage.

Institutional Growth

Strengthen the international capacity and identity of our school.

Mobility Activities

International learning opportunities for students and teachers

Student Internship Program

Fifteen students will participate in international internship programs to gain real business experience and professional competencies.

Six teachers will participate in job-shadowing activities to learn innovative teaching methods and educational practices.

Teachers

Institution

Stronger European identity, increased international visibility and improved educational quality.
